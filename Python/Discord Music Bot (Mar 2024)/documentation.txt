Commands: Prefix = "!"
join - make the bot join without playing audio
play [url] - play youtube video audio in voice channel
dc - make bot leave
skip - skip song
clear - clears queue and currently playing audio
pause - pause playing audio
resume - resume audio
queue - display current queue
ping - bot responds with pong. useful to check if bot is alive

*NOTE: When adding playlists make sure you send a link that looks like /playlist?list=XXX... not a playlist already being played which looks like watch?v=XXX&link?=XXX
